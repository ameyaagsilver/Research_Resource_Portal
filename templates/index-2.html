Welcome to Research Resource Portal

Made by the students of RV College of Engineering, Bengaluru

Students and teachers who are interested in research often don't have proper knowledge about the resources college possesses, thereby people have trouble locating down any kind of software tools or hardware components which will be required by them in their research work. So to make things easier, an online portal dedicated solely for the purpose of storing and displaying data of all the resources in the college which might be required in the research work.
